title: Browsing
description: Navigate your music library
---

bae provides several ways to explore your library.

## Views

### Albums

The default view shows all albums as a grid of cover art. Click an album to see its tracks.

### Artists

Browse by artist. Each artist shows their albums.

## Search

The search bar finds albums and artists instantly. Results appear as you type.

## Sorting

Sort your album view by:

- **Title** — Alphabetical by album title
- **Artist** — Alphabetical by artist
- **Year** — Chronological by release year
- **Date Added** — Newest imports first (default)

You can add multiple sort criteria and toggle ascending/descending for each.
